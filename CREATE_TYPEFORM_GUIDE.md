# Create Travyl Survey in Typeform - Step-by-Step Guide

## 🚀 Quick Setup (5-10 minutes)

### Step 1: Create Typeform Account

1. **Open browser** and go to: https://www.typeform.com
2. Click **"Sign up free"** (top right)
3. Sign up with:
   - Google account (fastest), OR
   - Email address
4. Verify email if needed

---

### Step 2: Create New Form

1. After signing in, click **"Create typeform"** (big blue button)
2. Choose **"Start from scratch"**
3. Click **"Create from scratch"** again
4. Name it: **"Travyl App Feature Ideas"**
5. Click **"Create"**

---

### Step 3: Add Questions (Copy-Paste These)

#### **Question 1: Frustrations**
- **Question type:** Long text
- **Question:** "What is your biggest frustration with planning travel?"
- **Required:** ✅ Yes
- Click **"Add"**

#### **Question 2: Feature Ideas**

  7. Social features - Share trips, see where friends went
  8. AI trip suggestions - Personalized recommendations
  9. Booking integration - Book flights/hotels directly
  10. Travel journal - Document memories with photos
  11. Real-time updates - Flight delays, gate changes
  12. Language translation - Built-in translator
  13. Currency converter - Easy money conversion
  14. Weather forecasts - Detailed weather info
  15. Other (add "Other" field ✅)

Click **"Add"**

### Step 4: Customize Design (Optional - 2 minutes)

1. Click **"Design"** (left sidebar)
2. **Theme:**
   - Choose a modern theme
   - Or click "Create theme" to customize
3. **Colors:**
   - Primary color: #4A90E2 (travel blue)
   - Background: White or light gray
4. **Font:**
   - Select "Modern" or "Clean"
5. **Add image:**
   - Click "Add image" at top
   - Upload a travel photo or use Unsplash
   - Search: "travel", "adventure", "vacation"

---

### Step 5: Settings (1 minute)

1. Click **"Settings"** (left sidebar, gear icon)
2. **General:**
   - ✅ Show progress bar
   - ✅ Show question numbers
3. **Responses:**
   - ✅ Limit to 1 response per person
   - ✅ Collect email addresses (optional)
4. **Confirmation:**
   - Message: "Thanks for your ideas! Your feedback will help us build a better travel app. 🌍✈️"

---

### Step 6: Preview & Test (1 minute)

1. Click **"Preview"** (top right)
2. Test all questions
3. Make sure:
   - Logic works correctly
   - Required questions are marked
   - Design looks good on mobile
4. Close preview

---

### Step 7: Share (30 seconds)

1. Click **"Share"** (top right)
2. **Copy link:**
   - Click "Copy link" button
   - Link format: `https://form.typeform.com/to/XXXXX`
3. **Share options:**
   - Direct link
   - Email invitation
   - Embed on website
   - QR code (for presentation slide)

---

## 📊 Viewing Results

### After people respond:

1. Go to your Typeform dashboard
2. Click on your form
3. Click **"Results"** (top)
4. View:
   - **Summary** - Overview stats
   - **Responses** - Individual answers
   - **Reports** - Charts and graphs
   - **Download** - Export to CSV/Excel

### For presentation:
- Screenshot the charts
- Show top features (Question 2)
- Share creative dream features (Question 9)
- Show pain points (Question 1)

## 💡 Pro Tips

### Make it more engaging:
1. **Add welcome screen:**
   - Click "+" at very top
   - Add "Welcome" screen
   - Text: "Help us design the ultimate travel app! 🌍 This takes 2-3 minutes."

2. **Add logic jumps:**
   - If they select "No" for group travel → Skip social features
   - If they select "None" for current apps → Ask what they'd want first

3. **Add images:**
   - Add travel photos to questions
   - Makes it more visual and engaging

4. **Use Piping:**
   - Reference their answers in later questions
   - Example: "You mentioned [Question 1 answer] - how would you solve this?"

## 🆘 Need Help?

**If you get stuck:**
- Typeform has excellent help docs: https://help.typeform.com
- Chat support available (bottom right corner)
- Video tutorials on YouTube

**Common issues:**
- Can't add question → Make sure you clicked "Add" after each question
- Missing question types → Click "More" to see all types
- Design won't change → Click "Apply" after changes
