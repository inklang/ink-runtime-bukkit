## How to Create This Form in Google Forms:

### Steps:
1. Go to **forms.google.com**
2. Click **"+"** to create a new form
3. Title: **"Travyl App User Feedback Survey"**
4. Description: *"Help us improve Travyl! Your feedback will directly influence our roadmap and help us build a better travel planning experience. This survey takes about 5-7 minutes to complete."*

### Settings to Enable:
- ✅ **Collect email addresses** (optional - for follow-up)
- ✅ **Limit to 1 response** (prevents duplicates)
- ✅ **Show progress bar**
- ✅ **Shuffle question order** (optional)
- ✅ **Confirmation message:** "Thank you for your feedback! Your input helps us make Travyl better for everyone."

### Sections:
Create 10 sections as outlined above

### Question Types:
- Rating questions: Use **Linear scale** (1-5)
- Multiple choice: Use **Multiple choice** or **Checkboxes**
- Open text: Use **Short answer** or **Paragraph**
- NPS: Use **Linear scale** (0-10)

### Required Questions:
Mark questions 4, 5, 6, 19, 35, 36 as **Required**
