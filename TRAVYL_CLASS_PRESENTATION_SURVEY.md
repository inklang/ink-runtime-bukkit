## Quick Setup Guide

### **Option 1: Google Forms (Easiest - 5 minutes)**

1. **Create form:**
   - Go to forms.google.com
   - Click "+" to create new form
   - Title: "Travyl App Feature Ideas"

2. **Add questions:**
   - Use this exact order
   - Question types:
     - Questions 1, 5, 9 → **Paragraph**
     - Question 2, 6 → **Checkboxes**
     - Question 3 → **Linear scale** (repeat 5 times)
     - Question 4, 7, 8 → **Multiple choice**

3. **Settings:**
   - ✅ Limit to 1 response
   - ✅ Show progress bar
   - Confirmation: "Thanks for your ideas! 🌍"

4. **Share:**
   - Click "Send"
   - Copy link
   - Share in class chat/presentation

**Total time:** 5 minutes

---

### **Option 2: Typeform (Better design - 10 minutes)**

1. **Create account:**
   - Go to typeform.com
   - Sign up free

2. **Create form:**
   - Click "Create typeform"
   - Start from scratch
   - Name: "Travyl App Feature Ideas"

3. **Add questions:**
   - Question 1 → **Long text**
   - Question 2 → **Multiple choice** (with "Other" option)
   - Question 3 → **Rating** (5 separate questions)
   - Question 4 → **Multiple choice**
   - Question 5 → **Long text**
   - Question 6 → **Multiple choice** (with "Other" option)
   - Question 7 → **Yes/No**
   - Question 8 → **Multiple choice**
   - Question 9 → **Long text** (optional)

4. **Add design:**
   - Upload travel-themed image
   - Choose modern colors

5. **Share:**
   - Click "Share"
   - Copy link

**Total time:** 10 minutes

## Presentation Tips

### **How to present the results:**

**Slide 1: Overview**
- "We surveyed X classmates about travel app features"
- "Here's what they want..."

**Slide 2: Top Features**
- Show top 5 most-wanted features (from Q2)
- Use bar chart or list

**Slide 3: Pain Points**
- Share 2-3 common frustrations (from Q1)
- "Most students struggle with..."

**Slide 4: Dream Features**
- Highlight 2-3 creative ideas (from Q5)
- "Students want innovative features like..."

**Slide 5: Travel Habits**
- Show trip types (from Q4)
- Age demographics (from Q8)

**Slide 6: Market Opportunity**
- "X% want group trip features"
- "Current apps don't solve Y problem"
- "Our app addresses these gaps"

## Expected Results

You'll likely see:
- **High demand for:** Price alerts, group planning, budget tracking
- **Common pain points:** Expensive flights, planning complexity, group coordination
- **Creative ideas:** AR navigation, AI assistants, social features

Use these insights to:
1. **Validate your app idea**
2. **Prioritize features**
3. **Show market demand** in your presentation
4. **Demonstrate user research** to professor
